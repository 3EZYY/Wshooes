Our Story, Your Comfort

From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in footwear innovation, Wshooes has been stepping forward since 2010.

Who We Are

Founded in 2010 by footwear enthusiast Mark Johnson, Wshooes began as a small workshop in Portland, Oregon. What started as a passion project crafting custom shoes has grown into an internationally recognized brand known for its commitment to comfort, quality, and style.

Today, we operate in over 30 countries with more than 200 retail stores worldwide, but we've never lost sight of our roots. Every pair of Wshooes still carries the same dedication to craftsmanship that marked our early days.

"Our mission has always been simple: create shoes that people love to wear. Not just for how they look, but for how they make you feel from the first step to the last."

- Mark Johnson, Founder

Our Journey
